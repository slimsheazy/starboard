#starboard: astrology-based divination app#

##Starboard is an astrology-based divination app designed for intuitive guidance, blending astrological transits, personal input, and algorithmically generated charms to provide insight.##

⸻

###Core Features:###

1️⃣ Minimalist Aesthetic
	•	OLED-style black background with twinkling stars.
	•	The app title, “starboard”, appears in lowercase when opened.
	•	No distractions, login, or unnecessary steps—straight into divination.

2️⃣ Astrological House System
	•	Uses a 12-house astrological wheel layout.
	•	Each house represents a domain of life and is always used in readings.
	•	Keywords assigned to each house for easy interpretation. 

3️⃣ Charm Board & Shake Interaction
	•	Users shake their phone to cast charms, triggering a glow surge effect.
	•	12 charms are cast onto the astrological wheel:
	•	8-9 core charms that appear every time.
	•	No fixed charm categories—each charm is multipurpose.
	•	Rare charms exist and are randomly triggered.
	•	Once cast, charms overlay the house keywords rather than replacing them.

4️⃣ User Input & Algorithmic Matching
	•	Users input:
	•	Specific question or focus area.
	•	The app analyzes:
	•	Current astrological transits.
	•	Lunar phase.
	•	Time of day.
	•	Algorithm selects 3-4 personalized charms based on user needs.

5️⃣ Charm System & Interpretation
	•	Charms provide symbolic guidance, blending astrology with intuitive messages. 

⸻

How It Works (Step-by-Step):

1️⃣ Open the app—no login required.
2️⃣ Input a question, focus, or emotional state.
3️⃣ Shake the phone or click to cast charms onto the astrological wheel.
4️⃣ Watch the glow surge and charms flicker into place.
5️⃣ View the charm placements within the 12 houses.
6️⃣ Interpret the overlapping keywords and charms for insight.

⸻

Overall Purpose & Vision

starboard is built for quick, intuitive divination, combining astrology, technology, and spiritual guidance. It eliminates unnecessary complexity, relying on direct cosmic messages through the charm system.
