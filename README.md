# starboard
an astrology based divination app

starboard is an astrology-based divination app that provides intuitive guidance by combining real-time astrological transits, lunar phases, and algorithmically generated charms. Designed for quick and insightful readings, starboard offers a minimalist, distraction-free experience that blends cosmic wisdom with interactive technology.

⸻

features

✨ minimalistic design
	•	no login or unnecessary steps—open the app and start divining.

🔮 astrological house system
	•	uses a 12-house astrological wheel layout.

🎲 charm casting & shake interaction 
	•	users shake their phone or click a button to cast 12 charms onto the astrological wheel.

🤖 algorithmic matching 
	•	uses current astrological transits and lunar phase for selection.
	•	users can input  their birth date for tailored results.

🃏 charm system & interpretation
	•	charms provide symbolic messages, blending astrology with intuitive guidance.
	•	rare charms exist and may appear randomly.

⸻

How It Works

1️⃣ Open the app – no login required.
2️⃣ Shake your phone or click the button to cast charms onto the astrological wheel.
3️⃣ Watch the glow surge and charms flicker into place.
4️⃣ Interpret the placements by reading house keywords and charm meanings.
